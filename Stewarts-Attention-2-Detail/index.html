Meet the Owner: Stewart G.

"I was born and raised in Tampa, graduated from King high school. I joined the Army and spent **22+ years serving my country.** I'm also retired from the civilian sector as well.

I've always had a passion for having a clean car. I started out detailing in some form or fashion **since 1974,** washing my brother's car just so I could drive it. Throughout my military career, I washed and detailed my soldiers', neighbors', and friends' vehicles weekly.

I decided to turn my passion into a business. Why not do something you enjoy doing and get paid for it? That's the passion and experience I bring to every vehicle."

- Stewart G., Owner & Operator
