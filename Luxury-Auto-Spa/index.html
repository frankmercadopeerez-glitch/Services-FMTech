Crafted Care Since 2025

Founded in 2025, Luxury Auto Spa began with a simple mission — to bring luxury-level detailing and protection to every vehicle. What started as a small, passion-driven operation quickly grew into a trusted name in premium auto care across the Bay Area.

We combine expert techniques with premium-grade products from P&S Detail Products and the Renny Doyle Double Black Collection to ensure every finish shines with perfection.

Meet the Founder

Founder Julio Orellana has been providing premium auto detailing since 2021. Certified through P&S University and an authorized installer of Double Black Renny Doyle Ceramic Coatings, Julio combines passion, precision, and professionalism in every service.
